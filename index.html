Best Dog Breeds for Pets

By Jacob Baird

Golden Retrievers

Best For: active families, cuddly kids

Description: Easy to train, very loving dogs. Origiating from Scotland, these playful and energetic dogs need a lot of attention and also normally come in three distinguishable colors!


Corgis

Best For: homey families, cuddly kids, older families

Description: These cute dogs are perfect for families with fewer commitments,as they are less energetic than most breeds. Their signature behinds are a staple in internet and irl comedy, and they are sure to make you laugh and smile all the time.


Pugs

Best For: loud families, cuddly kids, people on the lower energy side

Description: These adorable dogs are incredibly loving and funny. They have a slight breathing problem, but are still able to saunter along on a walk or jog!
